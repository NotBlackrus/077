Linking and Digital Media Integration

Building Digital Media Literacy

This assignment focuses on developing your skills in linking, embedding, and integrating YouTube content into digital projects. Understanding how to work with multimedia resources is essential in modern web development and digital communication.

You'll learn to navigate between platforms, understand URL structures, and create meaningful connections between different types of digital media - fundamental skills for any technology professional.
